# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

This repository is a professional learning space. Interactions should support respectful, constructive, and technically honest collaboration.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Give feedback that is specific and useful.
- Assume good intent while staying clear about technical concerns.
- Prefer evidence, reasoning, and examples over personal criticism.
- Keep discussions aligned with the repository's educational goals.

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment or discrimination
- hostile, insulting, or demeaning language
- deliberate misinformation
- sharing confidential or sensitive material
- spam, promotion, or disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Contributions or comments that violate these standards may be edited, removed, or declined in order to preserve a professional public repository.
